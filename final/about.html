    About BallisticsPro

    BallisticsPro is a web-based tool designed for hunters, competitive shooters, and firearms enthusiasts who need precise ballistics calculations for long-range shooting. Our calculator helps you compute bullet trajectories, drop, velocity, energy, and wind drift, taking into account factors like muzzle velocity, ballistic coefficient, bullet weight, and environmental conditions such as wind speed.

    The Science of Ballistics

    Ballistics is the science of projectile motion, involving factors like gravity, air resistance (drag), and wind. Understanding these forces allows shooters to predict where a bullet will land, ensuring accuracy over long distances. BallisticsPro uses a point-mass model to simplify these calculations, focusing on essential variables while providing reliable results for practical use.

    Why Use BallisticsPro?

    With BallisticsPro, you can:

    • Calculate accurate trajectories for various ammunition types.
    • Compare load profiles to choose the best setup for your shooting needs.
    • Adjust environmental factors in real-time to see their impact.
    • Export range cards for use in the field.

    Our goal is to empower shooters with the tools they need to improve accuracy and make informed decisions, all within a user-friendly interface.
